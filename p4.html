ABOUT · SUSTAIN

In order to leave the world a better place the aim of this young company is to provide integral environmental design proposals, and consultancy within the field of architecture and the built environment. With expertise in projects like Schiphol Airport, train station Amsterdam South, train station Stockholm Central and flood-protection in Tokyo, the company has much knowledge in the field of making integral designs for complex quests. Your venture with this company will therefore be secure, enjoyable and in line with your vision to also leave the world a better place.

· Urban Designs

· Interior Designs

FEATURED · FLOOD PROTECTION TOKYO · EDOGAWA-KU

Tokyo Gate Bridge · Tokyo Bay

Tokyo, a city densely populated with millions of people, is facing a challenge. The challenge that is called: 'climate change'. With the number of typhoons increasing and thereby the risk of tsunami's there is a need for smart and sustainable solutions in terms of water-protective measures.

Research on possible integral flood-protective measures to take in Tokyo's Edogawa-Ku was done for which the founder of SUSTAIN provided it's architectural expertise and worked in collaboration with professors and honors students of Delft University of Technology from other disciplines.

Consequently, a research article has been published in the journal Geosciences on the topic of interdisciplinary design methods to reduce flood-risk in Edogawa-Ku.
