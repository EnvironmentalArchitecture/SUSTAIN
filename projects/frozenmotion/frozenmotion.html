INTRODUCTION 路 FROZEN MOTION

Rotterdam, the Netherlands,

This project encompasses a proposal for a new modern art museum in the museum park of Rotterdam. The edge of park is a dyke. On this exact position a new art museum is proposed. The museum houses paintings, sculptures and more.

Artmuseum 路 Museumpark 路 Rotterdam

Rotterdam is known for its diverse architecture. The park is situated in the center of Rotterdam. The location can be approached from above the dyke and from below.

PROJECT PROPOSAL

A hi-tech engineering solution has been found for the structure of the building in order to create a building that seems to float and thereby intrigues the visitors.

"Frozen Motion"

The architecture looks like it wants to move since the floor slabs are slightly shifted from one another. This is supposed to ignite a level of excitement before one enters the building. Triggering a feeling of awe and understanding of the fact that art can move you and excite you too. One can feel inspired by art.

"Towards the Light"

"The windows of the building are completely covered by lamellas in order to protect the paintings for potential damage because of over exposure to light. Only at the head of the wings the windows are kept uncovered so that the visitors are directed to walk towards the light and enjoy the view at the end of the halls"
